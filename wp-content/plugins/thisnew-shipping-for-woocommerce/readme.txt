ThisNew will manage all the rest from production to delivery. ThisNew actualizes the perfect seller-buyer-factory collaboration.
We are entrusted to manufacture your high quality products and deliver them to your customers in a timely manner.

= How does ThisNew work? =
When a customer buys a product on your Shopify store, the order is imported to ThisNew, We are responsible for production,
quality check, packaging, delivery and will make sure the quality product reaches your customer in no time.
Connecting ThisNew with your Shopify store is easy:
Create your ThisNew and Shopify accounts
1.Click the “Add app” button at the top of this page and follow the steps.
2.Publish your designated products to your online store.
3.Synchronize your order, make payment at ThisNew, and you're done!

== Installation ==
1. Upload 'thisnew-shipping-for-woocommerce' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Click the "Connect" button or add your ThisNew API key manually to ThisNew->Settings tab
1. Enable shipping rate calculation in WooCommerce->Settings->Shipping->ThisNew Shipping tab
1. To automatically calculate taxes please check 'Enable taxes and tax calculations' under WooCommerce Tax settings.
1. Then go to ThisNew->Settings tab and check 'Calculated for all products shipped to states where ThisNew applies sales tax'.
